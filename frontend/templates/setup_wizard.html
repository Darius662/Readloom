Step 1: Create Your First Collection

Collections allow you to organize your manga/comics into groups and connect them to specific root folders.

Give your collection a descriptive name (e.g., "Manga Collection", "Comics Library")

Step 2: Add a Root Folder

Root folders are where Readloom will store and organize your manga/comic files.

Give your root folder a descriptive name
Enter the full path to the folder where your manga/comics will be stored (e.g., C:\Manga or /home/user/manga)
Select the primary type of content that will be stored in this folder
